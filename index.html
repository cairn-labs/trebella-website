Piano Lessons powered by Machine Learning

Trebella plugs into your electric piano or MIDI keyboard and listens as you play, comparing your style to that of thousands of other students and experts. Designed by an ex-Google engineer and a professional piano teacher, Trebella uses machine learning to offer critiques and practice tips just like a human teacher.
